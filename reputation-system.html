Reputation System:

The Reputation System is a point based system to form alliances between nations. This system will replace the existing "nation ally add" commands. Points, negative or postitive are awarded through player actions with the other nation. These actions can be commerce with the other nation, acts of hostiles such as kill citizens or other various actions to gain/lose points.

Reputation Level:

  • Hated
  • Hostile
  • Unfriendly
  • Neutral
  • Friendly
  • Honored
  • Revered
  • Exalted

Reputation Levels by Points:

  • - Hated = -36,000
  • - Hostile = -3,000
  • - Unfriendly = -3,000
  • - Neutral = 3,000
  • - Friendly = 6,000
  • - Honored = 12,000
  • - Revered = 21,000
  • - Exalted = 1,000

Reputation Awards by Level:

  • - Hated
    • - Inheritance: [Hostile]
    • - You are eligble to start a siege against the other nation.
  • - Hostile
    • - Inheritance: [Unfriendly]
    • - You cannot use the nation spawn of the other nation.
    • - You will automatically be flagged for PvP in all claims of that nation but you cannot start attacking first.
    • - You cannot longer buy, sell and interact with the other nations shops.
    • - Your nation will automatically become enemies of one another.
  • - Unfriendly
    • - You have a 50% surcharge in the nation spawn price.
  • - Neutral
    • - You can buy, sell and interact with the other nations shops.
    • - You cannot establish/buy plots from the other nation.
    • - You can use the nation spawn to the other nation for a price.
  • - Friendly
    • - Inheritance: [Neutral]
    • - You have a 50% discount in the nation spawn price.
  • - Honored
    • - Inheritance: [Friendly]
    • - You no longer have to pay to use their nation spawn.
    • - Your nation will automatically become allies of one another.
    • - You are eligble to merge with the one another.
  • - Revered
    • - Inheritance: [Honored]
    • - You can establish/buy plots from the other nation.
    • - Friendly Fire between your nations is disabled.
    • - You have a 100% discount in the nation spawn price.
    • - You can provide assistance at the other nation's sieges as an ally.
  • - Exalted
    • - Inheritance: [Revered]
    • - You can now use any town spawn of their nation.

Reputation Truce:

Sometimes issues are resolved but that may not be reflective in the reputation level if it stands below Neutral. This is why a Reputation Truce, an agreeable item conducted by both nation leaders can agree to reset their reputation level to neutral. A sort of "sue for peace" option.
